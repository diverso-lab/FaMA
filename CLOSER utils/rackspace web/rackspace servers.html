Pricing

We offer Cloud Servers in a range of sizes to suit your workloads and budget. You pay for only what you use, from the time you create a server until you delete it. We bill you for usage on a per-minute basis, rounding up to the next minute. We charge no minimum fees. The pricing below applies to Performance Cloud Servers residing in any of our US regions.


Every cloud account includes our Infrastructure Service Level with 24x7x365 access to Fanatical Support®. Upgrade to a Managed Cloud Service Level if you also need a dedicated team to help you architect, build, and manage your cloud. Learn more about our cloud service levels.

Bandwidth

Outgoing public

We charge for outgoing public bandwidth on a per-GB basis. To provide you the greatest savings, we aggregate outgoing public bandwidth (by account) for Cloud Servers, Cloud Files, Cloud Load Balancers, and Cloud Big Data across all US regions. We then apply the pricing tiers shown below to your aggregate usage.

Incoming public and internal (Cloud Networks and ServiceNet)

No charge.

Images

Custom image storage: $0.10/GB per month.

Images are compressed before they are stored. If you delete a Cloud Server, any images created from it will persist until you delete them.

1The number of virtual CPUs assigned to your Cloud Server. A vCPU corresponds to a physical CPU thread.

3The amount of additional disk space allocated for your application data. Data disks are RAID 10-protected and are NOT captured when you image a server.
 

4The amount of aggregate outbound bandwidth across all attached network interfaces (PublicNet, ServiceNet, Cloud Networks). The maximum amount of outbound public Internet bandwidth is limited to 40% of the aggregate limit. Inbound traffic is not limited. Host networking is redundant and bandwidth is delivered over two separate bonded interfaces, each able to carry 50% of the aggregate limit. We recommend using multiple Layer 4 connections to maximize throughput.
